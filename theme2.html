    Notes / FAQ

    Parent help
    What do these scores mean?
    This report is meant to be easy to understand. Higher numbers and fuller charts usually mean stronger performance or more completed practice. Missing sections are simply not shown when there is no data for that month.
    How can I help at home?
    Encourage short and regular English practice. Even 5–10 minutes a few times each week can help build confidence and improve long-term progress.
