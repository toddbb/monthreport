    Notes & FAQ

    Help
    What do these scores mean?
    This report is designed to be easy to understand. Higher numbers and fuller charts indicate stronger performance or more completed practice. Sections that are missing simply have no data available for this month.
    How are Language Skills assessed?
    Language Skills are based on the Teacher's assessment in every lesson. They reflect the student's current performance in class and are a key factor in determining their overall course progress. A score of 3 or above indicates the student is meeting expectations for their level, while scores of 1–2 suggest areas where additional focus and practice may be needed.
    How can I help at home?
    Encourage short and regular English practice. Even 5–10 minutes a few times each week can help build confidence and improve long-term progress.
    Who should I contact if I have questions?
    Please reach out to your child's Teaching Assistant or your Admissions Officer at your center.
    Can I view this report in English / Tôi có thể xem báo cáo này bằng tiếng Việt được không?
